Dual Dragon Karate

About Us

At Dual Dragon Karate, we aim to teach students how to defend themselves properly, perform technical martial arts techniques, learn discipline, and exercise all while having fun!

Classes

At Dual Dragon Karate, students will learn a variety of skills including but not limited to:

  • Self-Defense
  • Martial Arts Kata
  • Muscle Conditioning & Exercise
  • Sparring
  • Participating in Martial Arts Tournaments

Who Can Attend?

We welcome any students from ages 4 and up. Even if a student has a disability, we are ready and willing to accommodate their needs. Students are split into 2 classes: a kids class and a teen/adult class.

Pricing

The 1st class a student attends is FREE, Then after that, tution is $100 per student which include their 1st belt (white belt), and a uniform (or gi). Students are also encourged to acquired their own sparring gear through Dual Dragon Karate for $120.
